Join the Friends of Lums Pond at Volunteer Brewing Company Sunday April 19 for a 5K through Middletown. The proceeds from this run will go to enhance Lums Pond. Help us fundraise to put an adaptive kayak launch in at Lums Pond so that everyone can enjoy kayaking on the pond.

### Place

Volunteer Brewing Company<br/>
116 W Main Street<br/>
Middletown, DE US 19709

### Bib Pick Up and Registration

- Sunday April 19th from 8:00am till 8:55am at Volunteer Brewing.
- 5K Start Time 9am.

### Runners Amenities

Runners will receive:

- Race T-Shirt
- Post Race Snacks
- Volunteer Brewing Company Beer (21+)

### Age Groups and Awards

- Overall Male/Female
- Overall Masters Male/Female
- Top 3 in each age group Male/Female - medals
- 9 & under, 10-13, 14-19, 20-29, 30-39, 40-49, 50-59, 60-69, 70+
- _Overall and Masters - based off GUN TIME_
- _Age Groups - based off CHIP TIME_
