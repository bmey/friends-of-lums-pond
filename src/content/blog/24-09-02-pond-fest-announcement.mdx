### Volunteers Needed

VOLUNTEERS NEEDED for Pond Fest!
The plans are well under way and we have a great group of committee members with amazing ideas! BUT we are in need of several volunteers! Please consider volunteering for a couple hours at Pond Fest!

If you can spare some time that day, please fill out our [interest form here](https://forms.gle/41JAhPRMFMQ4jjDX9) or email <a href="mailto:pondfest@friendsoflumspond.org">pondfest@friendsoflumspond.org</a> to let us know! We would love to have you!
